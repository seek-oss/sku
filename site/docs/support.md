# Support

While sku is open source, its primary purpose is to improve consistency, cross-team collaboration and code quality at SEEK.

If you aren't building a SEEK UI, it may be worth considering alternatives such as [Vite] or [Remix].

## Getting help

sku is actively supported. We encourage people to reach out early rather than waiting until something becomes a blocker.

That includes:

- You've hit a bug or unexpected behaviour
- You have a new use case that might need a sku change
- Something feels awkward, confusing, or just not ideal
- You are thinking of using unsupported behaviour, or to bypassing restrictions

**If you work at SEEK**, please reach out in the [#sku-support](https://seekchat.slack.com/channels/sku-support) Slack channel.

[Vite]: https://vitejs.dev/
[Remix]: https://remix.run/
